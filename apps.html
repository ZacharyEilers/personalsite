School Chat

The Student Messaging Platform

School Chat allows anyone to connect with students from their high school simply by signing up and selecting their school. Using School Chat reduces the number of repeat questions directed at the teacher.

I built School Chat to allow students to collaborate on homework or answer general questions during pandemic.

Swim Stats

Search, Analyze, and Compare times from over 400,000 swimmers

Swim Stats allows parents, swimmers, and coaches to quickly compare swim times to standardized time cuts.

Swim Stats allows users to view the times from any swimmer within USA Swimming.

2019

Finished.

Homework Planning for Everyone

Finished allows users to plan their homework. Plan when to do it. Plan when it's due.

Finished was created to solve a commonly observed student problem, procrastination.

By deciding ahead of time exactly when homework was going to get done, students are less stressed, more focused, and perform better.

1700+ downloads

2018

Meteor Storm

The Best game

Meteor Storm is a mobile game played by saving the Earth from meteors flying towards it from the edges of the screen. The game keeps track of the user's high score, allowing players to compare their skills and compete for the best score.

I built Meteor Storm over about 3 months during January - March of my freshman year of high school. The game was built with the professional-grade Corona Game Engine.

990+ downloads
