Products

Manage your documents with our innovative, leading-edge family of products.

halFILE™ Document Manager

Our premier Document Manager. halFILE electronically converts paper documents into digital form then associates these images to a database record. It is now possible to store all of your company's documents on magnetic discs. So, a comprehensive computer database containing all of the vital information of those documents can be created. And, you can search for specific information that before may have taken hours or days to locate. The system includes powerful report writing capabilities, print capture, bar coding, optical character recognition and more.

halFILE™ Title Plant Manager

e.halFILE™ Internet Document Manager

Our web-based product provides for the search of halFILE documents on the Internet. Documents, captured by the halFILE Document Manager, can be searched for and viewed using e.halFILE and your browser. Transaction tracking, e.commerce, web-based database updates, powerful reporting, and security features are available.

halFILE CountyCashier

Designed for County Recorder office, halFILE CountyCashier records documents, receives payments and collects fees. Using one or more cash drawers, the system tracks money received and maintains an Accounts Receivable Ledger. A variety of user-definable reports are available including invoices, cash and account balances, and historical information. Fees can be allocated to General Ledger accounts. Documents received can be stamped using a slip printer and then scanned using halFILE Document Manager.

halFILE E-Mail Collector

halFILE E-mail Collector is an essential add-on application to halFILE to store and manage e-mail. Email documents are kept in their native format for easy retrieval but stored in a true document management system - halFILE! halFILE E-mail Collector manages e-mail from any Outlook / Exchange e-mail system in much the same way as halFILE manages documents. E-mail and it’s attachments are archived from the Outlook mailboxes into a halFILE database. All fields and attachments are saved and are immediately searchable using any halFILE search criteria.
